Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gokuldham.fun official?

No, Gokuldham.fun is an unofficial fan-made website created by a TMKOC enthusiast. We are not affiliated with the official show or its producers.

Are the games free to play?

Yes, all games on Gokuldham.fun are completely free to play. No downloads or payments required!

Can I suggest new game ideas?

Absolutely! We love hearing new game ideas from our community. Use the contact form above to share your suggestions.

About Gokuldham.fun

Gokuldham.fun is a fan-made tribute to the beloved Indian TV show "Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah". We create fun, free games inspired by the characters and stories from Gokuldham Society.

Our mission is to bring joy to TMKOC fans through interactive entertainment while respecting the original creators and copyright holders.
